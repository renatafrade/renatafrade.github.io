Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach integrates interdisciplinary research methodologies with practical applications, emphasizing:

  • •Participatory and horizontal learning environments that value diverse forms of knowledge
  • •Feminist pedagogies centered on inclusion, empowerment, and critical digital literacy
  • •Research-driven teaching connecting theoretical frameworks with empirical applications
  • •Science communication making complex research accessible to diverse audiences
  • •Social responsibility in technology development and research practice
