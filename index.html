Renata Loureiro Frade, PhD

Dr. Renata Frade is an interdisciplinary researcher in Communication Sciences, Information Science, and Digital Technologies working at the intersection of technology, gender, and artificial intelligence. With a PhD in Information and Communication in Digital Platforms from the University of Aveiro and University of Porto (awarded with Distinction and Honours), her research focuses on feminist media studies, digital storytelling, and ethical AI.

She is a pioneer in developing technofeminist storytelling as a communicational praxis, examining how AI systems reproduce or contest gendered inequalities through narrative, visibility, and symbolic recognition. Her work combines feminist HCI, participatory design, and critical platform studies to understand women's technology communities in Lusophone contexts.
